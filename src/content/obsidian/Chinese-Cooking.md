## Essentials

[[How to Stock a Chinese Pantry Essential Staples to Keep on Hand]]

* Chinkiang Vinegar/Black Rice Vinegar
* Toasted Sesame Oil

* Oyster Sauce
  * Kee Kum Kee Premium Oyster-Flavored Sauce
* Dried Chilies
* Dried Mushrooms
